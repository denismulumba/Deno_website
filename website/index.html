Learn About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computing (Computational Mathematics Science and Engineering) at Boise State University, with an expected graduation in Fall 2026. I hold an M.Sc. in Mathematical Sciences from AIMS, Rwanda, with a focus on climate modeling and data assimilation, and a B.Sc. in Mathematics and Physics from Makerere University, Uganda. My work has been recognized with several competitive awards, including the ACS Fall 2024 Best Oral Presentation Award, the CASCADE RAIN Travel Award, the Boise State GEM Scholarship, and a Graduate Research Assistantship. I am also a recipient of the AIMS Master’s Scholarship (Mastercard Foundation) and the Uganda Government National Merit Scholarship.
